Old dreams don't die.
They just get louder.

WHEN THE BASS DROPS is about a 40 year old woman on a downward spiral of life who dares to claim her teenage dream of being a hip hop dancer in NYC and winds up finding self-worth. Her love of old school hip hop and passion for dancing to it takes her above life's darkest challenges to a love of self.

We are seeking financing for this exciting, heartfelt movie.

On board the project so far: DJ Red Alert. Nice and Smooth. Stetsasonic. Mark the 45 King. Ralph McDaniels (Video Music Box). Please support the film by liking it on Facebook, and be on the lookout for the crowdfunding campaign coming this January. Check in for updates.
